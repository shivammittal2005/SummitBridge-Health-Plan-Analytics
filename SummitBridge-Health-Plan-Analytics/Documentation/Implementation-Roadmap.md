# Implementation Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Validate and Govern | 0–3 Months

- Validate the high-cost-member list.
- Review ED classification logic.
- Confirm PCP attribution.
- Validate Specialty Rx opportunities.
- Establish baselines, owners and clinical guardrails.

## Phase 2 — Launch Measurable Pilots | 3–6 Months

- Launch a high-cost-member care pod.
- Pilot strictly avoidable ED navigation.
- Begin targeted Specialty Rx review.
- Start Marketplace retention outreach.

## Phase 3 — Expand Based on Pilot Learning | 6–12 Months

- Expand post-triage ED routing.
- Add behavioral-health navigation.
- Strengthen discharge follow-up.
- Address targeted network and access gaps.

## Phase 4 — Scale Advanced Capabilities | 12+ Months

- Predictive high-cost risk stratification.
- Value-based specialty contracts.
- Closed-loop care navigation.
- AI-enabled workflow support, subject to governance and validated data quality.

## Decision Rule

Scale only when:

- Net savings remain positive after program cost.
- Clinical and member-experience guardrails remain stable.
- Operational capacity exists.
- Data quality and attribution are sufficiently reliable.
