# Savings Scenarios

## Direct, Measurable Range

The strongest defensible planning range combines ED site-of-care shifts and Specialty Rx optimization.

| Scenario | Calculation | Gross Opportunity |
|---|---|---:|
| Direct Base | 20% of 613 strictly avoidable ED claim lines × ~$992 + 10% Rx optimization | ~$201K |
| Expanded ED + 10% Rx | 20% of 914 ED candidates × ~$992 + 10% Rx optimization | ~$260K |
| Combined Upside | 20% of 914 ED candidates × ~$992 + 15% Rx optimization | ~$300K |

## Broader Five-Lever Scenario

The broader scenario adds:

- 10% reduction in top-5% cohort spend
- Expanded ED diversion
- 15% Specialty Rx optimization
- Behavioral-health-related ED opportunity

This produces approximately **$652.9K**, rounded to **$653K**, before program cost and pilot validation. Retention value is not monetized.

## Integrated Operating Upside

The **$1.2M** scenario is an illustrative integrated operating case using stronger assumptions, including higher ED diversion and reduced high-cost escalation.

It should not be presented as the validated base case.

## Required Language

Use:

- Gross opportunity
- Scenario-based estimate
- Before program cost
- Subject to clinical validation
- Illustrative operating upside

Avoid:

- Saved
- Guaranteed
- Realized impact
- Proven reduction
